FAQs

Is Shopify Magic free or part of a Shopify subscription plan?
Shopify Magic tools and experiences are currently available for free to all Shopify merchants, regardless of their subscription plan. However, the access and availability of specific features may vary.
_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Is my data secure with Shopify Magic?
Yes. Shopify Magic is built on Shopify’s robust security architecture and privacy policy trusted by millions of companies around the world. Learn more about how Shopify handles data here.
_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Can I use Shopify Magic on mobile?
Not all features powered by Shopify Magic are optimized for mobile at this time.
_____________________________________________________________________________________________________________
How many languages does Shopify Magic support?
Language support for Shopify Magic may vary by feature. Most features support English, however, there are features that support additional languages namely English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Portuguese (Brazilian), Chinese (Simplified). For more information, please consult the help docs on Shopify Magic here.

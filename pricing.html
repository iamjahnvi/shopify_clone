FAQs

What is Shopify and how does it work?
Shopify is an all-in-one commerce platform that lets you create an online store, accept payments, and sell on social channels and in person—all from one dashboard. Build a professional storefront, manage products and inventory, process orders and shipping, automate marketing and SEO, analyze sales, and run your business from web or month.
How much does Shopify cost?
Shopify starts with a free 3-day trial—no credit card required. After your trial, most plans start at just ₹20 per month for the first 3 months, then switch to standard monthly pricing. Additionally, you can choose to add POS Pro to your subscription for more advanced point of sale features like staff roles and permissions, exchanges, and flexible customer pickup and delivery options.
Can I cancel or change my subscription anytime?
Yes. If you ever decide that Shopify isn't the best commerce platform for your business, simply cancel your account. If you choose a 1- or 3-year Plus subscription, you can cancel when your term commitment expires.
Are there any additional fees?
There are no setup fees on any of our plans. Third-party transaction fees may apply when you use a third-party payment provider (instead of Shopify Payments). The rate depends on your plan. Shopify charges a small fee to allow you to accept major credit cards, such as Visa and Mastercard, as payment in your store. These fees vary depending on the plan that you choose.
In what countries can I use Shopify?
Shopify is available in nearly every country in the world. Some features vary by country—for example, Shopify Payments is available only in certain countries—so pricing and payment options can differ depending on where your business is based.
Is Shopify PCI Compliant or PCI Certified?
Yes. Shopify is certified Level 1 PCI DSS compliant, and stores powered by Shopify are PCI compliant by default, which helps keep payment information secure.
Can I use my own domain name with Shopify?
Yes. You can use a domain you already own, or buy a new one. Every Shopify store also comes with a free myshopify.com address, and Shopify helps keep your site secure automatically. If you already have a domain, you can keep it where you bought it and simply connect it to Shopify—or move it to Shopify so everything is managed in one place.
Do I get web hosting with Shopify?
Yes. Shopify includes secure commerce hosting with your Shopify plan, so you don't need to buy separate hosting. Shopify also includes unlimited bandwidth (no bandwidth fees) and provides a free TLS/SSL certificate for domains you add to Shopify. Additionally, you can use the Buy Button sales channel to embed buy buttons on an external website or blog, and let customers check out using Shopify Checkout.
